About Me

I’m a researcher and PhD candidate at Técnico Lisboa, focusing on deep learning for retinal image analysis and biomedical image segmentation. My work involves developing and evaluating advanced convolutional and transformer-based architectures to enhance retinal layer segmentation and improve clinical interpretation.

I am passionate about combining computational modeling with medical research to bridge the gap between AI and clinical applications.

Education

  • MSc in Biomedical Engineering — Técnico Lisboa (2023)

Research Interests

  • Deep learning for medical imaging
  • Retinal layer segmentation
  • Representation learning and self-supervised methods
