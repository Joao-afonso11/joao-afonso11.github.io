About Me

I’m a researcher at the Interactive Technologies Institute, Lisbon, focusing on topics including Deep Learning image segmentation and disease classification of retinal diseases. Furthermore, I work with Unity to develop annotation surfaces suitable for ophthalmologists to label and identify specific structures in their scans.
